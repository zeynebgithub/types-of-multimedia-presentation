Types of Multimedia Presentation

Multimedia Presentation definition

A multimedia presentation would be a presentation file which is not limited to just text in terms of content. A multimedia CD presentation creates visual impact, and you can include details like industry information, company prospectus, business goal, product details and catalogues, just about everything you want to! Typically, a multimedia presentation contains at least one of the following elements: video or movie clip, animation, sound, links, images, animated gifs, video.​

There are 2 types of Multimedia:

  • Linear Multimedia
  • Nonlinear Multimedia

Linear Multimedia

Linear Multimedia is a type of a multimedia that is designed to be presented in a sequential manner. ​ It has a distinct beginning and end. It goes on a logical flow from a starting point to a conclusion. In this kind of presentation, the creator of the multimedia is in control.​ Main goals include: to entertain, to transmit knowledge, and to make people familiar on a certain topic Without any form of diversion.​

Examples of Linear Multimedia

  • A powerpoint presentation​
  • An anime episode​
  • A slideshow of pictures that goes on with a specific direction​
  • A Youtube video​
  • A storyline/ A movie​

Advantages and Disadvantages of Linear Multimedia
Advantages Disatvantages
Audience gets to focus and concentrate on a specific topic.​ Minimal interactivity, or none at all​
There is logical order in the presentation. Organized​ Audience has no say on the topic they want to dwell into.

Nonlinear Multimedia

Non-linear multimedia is a non-sequential type of multimedia where the person’s participation is crucial.​ In this type of media, the person needs to interact with a computer program, thus making him in control of the experience.​ With the presence of an interface, the person and the computer interacts with each other.​

Examples of Nonlinear Multimedia

  • A website​
  • A search engine’s home page​​
  • A DVD menu screen​
  • An anime or Korean drama streaming site​
  • A Youtube Channel​

Advantages and Disadvantages of Nonlinear Multimedia
Advantages Disatvantages
The person is in control and may use the multimedia according to his preferences and needs.​​ Requires a level of computer literacy from the user​. May be unorganized if not used well.


What is the difference between Linear and Nonlinear Multimedia

Linear active content progresses without any navigational content for the viewer. Cinema presentation is an example of linear multimedia.​
Non-linear content offers user interactivity to control progresses as used with a computer game.Hypermedia is an example of non-linear multimedia.
